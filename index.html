Tiara Stinson Lavender

About Me: Hi! I'm a library assistant breaking into the UX field. I'm pursuing a master’s degree at Michigan State University with the expected graduation date of Spring 2025. I love to dig into customer’s technology problems and solving them through user-friendly designs.
Off the clock, I like trying new things and being creative, but the hobbies that stuck are cooking and crocheting. I also watch a little anime and cosplay.

Skills: User research, UX design, wireframing, prototyping, Figma, HTML, CSS.

I Excel In:

  • Putting people first - good design is functional, but the best designs are the ones that are functional and user-centric.
  • Adapting - I keep calm and pivot when plans change.
  • Storytelling - whether I’m presenting to different teams or to stakeholders, I make sure my findings are well-understood by all audiences.
